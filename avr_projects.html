AVR Microcontroller Projects App

Atmel(Microchip) Studio Projects and Proteus Simulations for AVR Microcontroller, specifically ATMega328p. I developed AVR Microcontroller Projects in 2 separate apps: FREE and PREMIUM. In FREE version, some of the projects are locked, which means that users have to purchase premium edition if they need to get access to locked projects.

Core Features

  • Complete C Language implementation of sensors and actuators with ATMega328p and Atmel Studio
  • Datasheets collection
  • Electronic Calculators

Tech Stack

  • Flutter
