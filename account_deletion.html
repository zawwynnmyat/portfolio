Account Deletion – Green Globe

This page explains how users of Green Globe can delete their account and associated personal data. Account deletion is available directly within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Green Globe account by following these steps:

  • Open the Green Globe app
  • Go to Account Tab and click About.
  • Click Delete Account
  • Re-enter your password and confirm the deletion

What Data Is Deleted

Once your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User account information
  • App usage data and preferences
  • Any content associated with your account

Data Retention

All personal data is deleted immediately or within a reasonable period after the deletion request is confirmed. Certain information may be retained if required by applicable laws or regulations.
