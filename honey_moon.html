Honeymoon

Honeymoon is a dating platform developed for a client, with Flutter and Firebase as a backend service, in March & April 2023.

Core Features

  • Tinder-like swappable cards (swipe LEFT to cancel, swipe RIGHT to add to the favorite list, swipe TOP or BOTTOM to skip and UNDO feature) in dating feed
  • Age and Gender (Male, Female & Both) Filters in App Settings
  • Real-time chatting which allows user to send text messages, photos and even files.
  • Dark Mode and Light Mode in chatting, Delete chats
  • Users’ online active status
  • Block / Unblock other users
  • Editable user profiles with a wide variety of options to alter
  • “Want To Meet”, a feature which allows users to get to know who wanted to meet them, and “You Liked It”, a feature which enables users to keep track of other user profiles they liked.
  • In FREE version, user profile pictures in “Want To Meet” session is blurred and they have to view Video Ads whenever they click the profile; however, when a user purchased PREMIUM version, the blurred photos will be unblurred.
  • Report policy violated users
  • Google AdMob Integrated

Tech Stack

  • Flutter for cross-platform development
  • Firebase as a backend service
