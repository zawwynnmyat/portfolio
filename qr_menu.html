Digital QR Menu

Digital QR Menu was made for Suprise Tea, a tearoom based in Mandalay, Myanmar.

Core Features: (Scan That QR Code to see the features)

  • Order Tea and other foods (with 2 options: in-person & eat-later)
  • Search food & drinks
  • (Admin Side) Update/add/delete prices/discounts & item details with ease
  • (Admin Side) Receiving customer orders
    • (Admin Side) Printing receipt with Bluetooth thermal printer

Tech Stack

  • Flutter Web
  • Firebase as a backend service
  • Dart
  • Bluetooth Thermal Printing
  • Redux for State Management
  • Hive for Local Database
