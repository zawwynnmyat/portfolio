Rainbow House

This app was developed for a client who is selling painting products in Mandalay, Myanmar. His shop, Rainbow House, is distributing a wide variety of paints and related accessories in retail and wholesale.I developed 2 apps: one for customers of Rainbow House and another one for the owner of Rainbow House.

Core Features:

  • Explore paints for different brands
  • Prices and discounts depend on the situation whether customer is a retailer or not. If a user can provide an authorized retailer code, the app marks the account as retailer account; otherwise, he or she is normal user.
  • Prices totally depends the volume (eg: 1 liter, 5 liters, 18 liters, etc.) and the intensity of paint color.
  • Color Bank with over thousands of valid painting colors, with a function to inspire their home (eg: virtualized painting their bathroom or living room or kitchen).
  • Saving their favorite colors and the feature to share these colors with their colleagues
  • Shopping Cart
  • Stock number validation
  • Categorized Paints and related accessories
  • Searching Products
  • Order Tracking
  • Manage customer orders (Admin Side)
  • Generate Authorized Retailer Code (Admin Side)
  • Add/Edit paints and related accessories (Admin Side)

Tech Stack

  • Flutter for cross-platform development
  • Firebase as a backend service
  • Dart
  • Bluetooth Thermal Printing
  • Redux for State Management
  • Hive for Local Database
