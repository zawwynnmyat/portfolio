Live4D Results

Live 4D Result is an app which allows users to view Malaysia 4D results in real time. The app was developed for a client from Malaysia in February 2023. It also includes Google AdMob integration.

Core Features

  • Explore 4D Results of Singapore 4D, Magnum, Damacai, Toto, Sabah 88, Sarawak, Sandakan, Grand Dragon, and 9 Lotto
  • Lucky Number and Bookmarking favorite numbers
  • Number History
  • Notifications

Tech Stack

  • Flutter for cross-platform development
