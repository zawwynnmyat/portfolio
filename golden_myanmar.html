Golden Myanmar : Exchange Rates

Golden Myanmar is a mobile app which can be used to know real world exchange rates, especially Malaysian Ringgit to Burmese Kyat, in different exchange counter in real time. It was developed for a burmese client from Malaysia. Moreover, It also has local news features, and integrated Google AdMob.

Core Features

  • Exchange Rates & Exchange Rate Calculator (Ringgit to Kyat, and vice versa)
  • Local News

Tech Stack

  • Flutter for cross-platform development
