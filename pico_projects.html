Pico Projects

MicroPython projects for Raspberry Pi Pico (RP2040 Microcontroller). I developed PICO Projects in 2 separate apps: FREE and PREMIUM. In FREE version, some of the MicroPython projects are locked, which means that users have to purchase premium edition if they need to get access to locked projects.

Core Features

  • Complete MicroPython implementation of sensors and actuators with RP2040 Microcontroller
  • Datasheets collection
  • 7 Segment Display Calculation

Tech Stack

  • Flutter
