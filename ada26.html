Bronze Award in the Digital Startup Category at the ASEAN Digital Awards 2026 — awarded to Green Globe in Hà Nội, Vietnam (January 14–15).

This recognition reflects months of dedication to building technology that supports sustainability and environmental impact across Southeast Asia. Green Globe was created with a simple mission: to use digital innovation to help communities and organizations make smarter, greener decisions.

Being acknowledged on a regional stage alongside inspiring innovators is both humbling and motivating. This milestone strengthens my commitment to continue developing scalable, purpose-driven solutions through technology.

Grateful for the support of mentors, collaborators, and everyone who believed in the vision. The journey is just getting started.
