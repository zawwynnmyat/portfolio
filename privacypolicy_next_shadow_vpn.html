Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Next Shadow VPN ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The App operates entirely on your device without transmitting any user data to our servers. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Browsing history or activity logs
  • IP addresses
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or telemetry

3. VPN Connection

The App establishes a VPN connection to servers you configure. All network traffic passes through the VPN tunnel directly between your device and the configured server.

We do not operate, monitor, or log traffic on any VPN servers. The server configurations (addresses, credentials) are stored locally on your device only.

4. Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Server configurations you add (address, port, protocol settings)
  • Subscription URLs you add
  • App preferences (theme, DNS settings, routing rules)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

5. Network Permissions

Next Shadow uses Android's VpnService to create an encrypted network proxy server, ensuring secure and private internet browsing.

The use of VpnService is essential for providing the core VPN functionality, allowing Next Shadow to securely route your network traffic through a protected connection, even on public or shared networks.

This feature safeguards your data privacy and enhances security while you browse online.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, crash reporting services, or any similar tools.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect any personal information from any user, there is no risk of inadvertently collecting children's data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the App update. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
